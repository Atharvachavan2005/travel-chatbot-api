Trip Name: Ladakh Monastery Explorer
Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Leh. Acclimatize to the high altitude. Rest day.
Day 2: Leh local sightseeing. Visit Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, and the local market.
Day 3: Excursion to Sham Valley. Visit Magnetic Hill, Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, and Sangam (Confluence of Indus & Zanskar rivers).
Day 4: Travel to Nubra Valley via Khardung La, one of the world's highest motorable passes.
Day 5: Explore Nubra Valley's Diskit Monastery and Hunder sand dunes. Enjoy a double-humped camel ride.
Day 6: Return to Leh. Evening free for leisure.
Day 7: Departure from Leh airport.

Inclusions: Accommodation, Breakfast & Dinner, All permits, Oxygen cylinder in vehicle.
Exclusions: Flights, Lunch, Personal expenses.

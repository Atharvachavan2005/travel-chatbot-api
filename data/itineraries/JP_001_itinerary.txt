Trip Name: Royal Rajasthan
Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur. Check into the hotel. Evening visit to Chokhi Dhani for a traditional Rajasthani dinner.
Day 2: Full-day city tour. Visit Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and the Jantar Mantar observatory.
Day 3: Explore the magnificent Amer Fort. Enjoy an elephant ride and the light & sound show.
Day 4: Day trip to the local markets for shopping. Explore Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar.
Day 5: After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to the airport for departure.

Inclusions: Hotel accommodation, Daily breakfast, All sightseeing as per itinerary, AC vehicle.
Exclusions: Flights, Lunch & Dinner, Entry fees for monuments.
